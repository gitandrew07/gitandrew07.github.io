my fav albums


IGOR

IGOR is by far, my favourite album by Tyler, The Creator, and also my fav album of all time
This is also the first album that got me into his music, from there it was like a new world
opened for me. I reccommend a listen to everyone, its full of different genres, not only rap.
My favourite track on there is "I THINK".


CHROMAKOPIA

Tyler, The Creator's latest album is really touching, it was the first album rollout of his that
i have experienced, it was fun seeing all of the teasers and adverts around social media.
We could say this is a really personal album, talking about Tyler's insecuritites and other
topics. My favourite song from here is probably "I Hope You Find Your Way Home", literally brings
me to tears.


Mr. Morale and The Big Steppers

This is my favourite Kendrick Album; personally, its really overhated, a lot of songs on here
are straight vibes. Of course it was something no one expected, a personal album from Kendrick
5 years after "DAMN.". Besides, i think this album is really touching to me, tracks like Mother I Sober,
and my favourite, "Mirror", literally would get me into tears.


GNX

Kendrick's latest album literally dropped without warning, 30 minutes after its teaser was posted.
It was something again that no one expected, this mixtape vibe really separates the album from its
past projects. The result is personally his hardest album to date. My favourite tracks are "hey now",
"peekaboo" and "gnx".
